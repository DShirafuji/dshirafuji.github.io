Biography

I am a researcher at Mitsubishi Electric Corporation. My research interests lie mainly in Natural Language Processing (NLP), especially in argument mining, social bias mitigation, and multi-modal retrieval.

From April 2024, I have collaborated with Prof. Yasutomo Kimura and his lab at Otaru University of Commerce.
